Powerball Simulator

This program is a windows forms application that simulates play of the multi-state Powerball lottery game http://www.powerball.com/games/home It randomly picks the "winning" white ball numbers (5) and the winning red ball (1). It then randomly draws 5 white balls and 1 red ball and checks to see if these match the "winning" numbers. It continues drawing until the draw matches the winning numbers.
